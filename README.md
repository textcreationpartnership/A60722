1. Sodom Fair:
OR THE
MARKET
OF THE
MAN of SIN.

    _ A true and real Account of the Pope's Pardons and
Dispensations, with their Prices, as they are sold
in his Holynesse's Spiritual Court.

      * Dispensations for Drunkards.

      * Dispensations for such as have been, or are to be
promoted, being under Age.

      * For Lieenses to take Orders.

      * Dispensations for such as are dismembered, or want
some of their Limbs, to take Orders.

      * Dispensations for Wilful Murther.

      * For Murdering of Priests.

      * For killing a Lay-Man.

      * For Murdering of Children.

      * For Charming and Witchcraft.

      * For Hereticks.

      * For Sacriledge, Theft, Fireing Houses, Rapes, Perjury,
and such-like Sins.

      * For Frailty of the Flesh.

      * Of Diverse Transgressions.

    _ The History of Fornication and Adultery.

    _ The Life of Pope Clement the Sixth, with a Limb
of his Bull of Blasphemies; which he Published for
the year of Iubele, 1350.
